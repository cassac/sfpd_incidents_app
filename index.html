Welcome

This application queries SF's Open Data API for local incident reports from the police department. View distribution of hourly, daily, and monthly distribution of incidents and individual reports in tabular data format.

Instructions

  • Results use zero-based indexing. Day 0 is Monday and month 3 is April. Time is 24 hour based. Hour 14 is 2:00pm.
  • Use the form on the left to filter data. No selections will return results from every category/date/time.
  • Providing both dates/times will perform a "between" query while providing one (beginning or ending) will perform a "less than" or "greater than" query.
  • Selecting output type "tabular data" with a limit over 1000 results may temporarily freeze your browser.
  • The month view for the "distribution graph" is inaccurate for broad searches because too many incidents are returned from the most recent months.
